Blog shortcuts

Preview

Make a preview

$ pandoc post.md > preview.html

Publishing

Then make a post

$ pandoc post.md > post.html; \
  cat post.md | grep -oP ' #[[:alnum:]]+' | grep -oP '#[[:alnum:]]+' > hashtags.txt

Remove publishing

Then make a post

$ rm post.html
